## Legitimate use (read first)

These blogs are **third-party publications**. Their photography is owned by either the blog or — more often — the wedding photographers who shot the events.

**Do not:**
- Scrape and republish images
- Copy stories with light rewrites
- Use the blog's logos or brand marks

**Do:**
- Use them as **topic radar** (what's trending, what couples are searching for)
- Link out with attribution in roundup posts
- Reach out for licensing or content partnerships
- Source imagery from licensed stock (Unsplash, Pexels, Adobe Stock) or commission/AI-generate your own
